Our mission at Orlladelley Aluminium & Sundry is to provide our customers with access to
authentic, unique, and eco-friendly buildings and homes.
We’re experts at maximizing the use of space to create beautiful,
unique buildings that contribute to the business world.

Orlladelley Aluminium & Sundry is one of the country's leading aluminium construction companies and
is built on two decades of experience. Born in 2000 from a
love of clean lines combined with exceptional durability and craftmanship.
AOS is the pinnacle of Nigerian style combined with modern innovation.

If you’re intrigued by the sleek, efficient, and eco-friendly beauty of acceptable design,
we’re thrilled to offer you the option to design and build the
AOS building of your dreams.

Eco-Luxury

At Orlladelley Aluminium & Sundry, we stand for change.

Traditionally, aluminium construction supplies are sourced from rare and costly materials, solely focused on attainability — not sustainability.

We wanted to offer a better solution. By reducing our carbon footprint, eliminating the use of toxic glues, and sourcing our materials responsibly, we’re proud to provide quality building materials that don’t compromise your well-being.
